Instruction :

  • Normal Mode :

    Normal traditional tetris concept

  • Hard Mode :

    Speed of the tetris will increase base on number of lines.

  • Swipe left :

    Move block to the left side (1 block at a time)

  • Swipe right :

    Move block to the right side (1 block at a time)

  • Swipe down :

    Move block to the down side (1 block at a time)

  • Tap screen :

    Rotate the current block being played (clockwise)
